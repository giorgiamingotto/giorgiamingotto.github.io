about me

My name is Giorgia Mingotto, and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Architecture from IUAV in Venice. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Architecture (Conservation) at Sapienza University of Rome. Throughout my studies, I have developed a strong interest in the restoration and preservation of architectural heritage, while also focusing on the integration of new technologies in architecture. I firmly believe that the combination of tradition and innovation can offer creative and sustainable solutions for the restoration of historic buildings. In my projects, I strive to balance respect for the past with the use of advanced tools and methodologies, such as digital modeling and sustainable technologies, to breathe new life into buildings and improve the quality of living spaces.
